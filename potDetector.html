This data is taken from a cosmic ray detector test stand located in Atlanta, GA. It is in 1 Park Place, Rm. 706. The graph shows the % variation in cosmic rays detected per hour using a running average. The span of the running average can be adjusted using the slider.

This test stand records data from a detector that uses a scintillating liquid sensitive to both muons and neutrons. The above graph shows the combined counts of both muons and neutrons.
